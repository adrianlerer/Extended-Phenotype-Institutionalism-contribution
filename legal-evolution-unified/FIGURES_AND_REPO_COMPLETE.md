## ðŸŽ“ Theoretical Framework

### Extended Phenotype Theory (Dawkins 1982)

**Applied to International Law:**
- **Memeplexes:** Globalist vs Sovereigntist ideological frameworks
- **Phenotypes:** Legal institutions (courts, treaties, norms)
- **Selection Pressure:** Crisis conditions vs stable conditions
- **Fitness:** (Institutional Persistence Ã— Goal Achievement) / Adaptation Cost
- **Transmission:** Citation networks as memetic vectors

### Propensity Score Matching (Rosenbaum & Rubin 1983)

**Causal Inference Method:**
- Treatment: Crisis-catalyzed legal conflicts
- Control: Baseline cooperation cases
- Matching: On observable covariates (geography, legal family, year)
- Estimand: Average Treatment Effect on Treated (ATT)
- Balance check: Standardized Mean Differences (SMD < 0.1 target)
