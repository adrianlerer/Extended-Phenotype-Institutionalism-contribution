cumulative slopes. Legal institutions exhibit neither intelligent design nor purely 
gradualist evolution, but rather the hybrid pattern characteristic of complex 
adaptive systems. Understanding these evolutionary constraints clarifies why 
certain reforms succeed while others fail and why constitutional structures resist 
transplantation across different environments.
