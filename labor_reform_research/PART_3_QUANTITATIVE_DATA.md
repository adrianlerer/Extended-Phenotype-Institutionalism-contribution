### B. Time to Resolution (Median Duration in Months)

| Country | Pre-Reform | Post-Reform | Improvement | Mechanism |

| **ARGENTINA** | 36-48 months (2010) | **42-54 months** (2024) | ❌ +10-15% | **Getting WORSE** |

**Argentina Deep Dive:**

**Litigation Timeline (typical case):**
1. **Filing → First hearing:** 6-12 months (backlog)
2. **Evidence phase:** 12-18 months (delays, adjournments)
3. **Expert witnesses:** 6-12 months (technical reports)
4. **First instance ruling:** 30-36 months total
5. **Appeals (Labor Chamber):** +12-18 months
6. **Cassation (CSJN if applicable):** +24-36 months

**Total:** 4.5 years (first instance) to **8+ years** (if appealed to CSJN)
